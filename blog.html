The journey to develop this site.

The journey began from the day I took off from college after completing my graduation with a dream of developing my own website. When I entered the Industry I started off with mobile application development in iOS and Android, later when I was presented with a requirement to start off with Hybrid mobile application development for its huge time and cost effective property, I realized, it would not be an easy task to dive straight away in it without acquiring convenient knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3.

I figured it out as an opportunity to build my website and snag complete knowledge of web designing. I took the firt step of learning through W3C website which eventually made me switch into Adobe Dreamweaver using video tutorials from youtube. The first few days were pretty tough to get along with Dreamweaver as the tool and the UI was quite tough with respect to accuracy. I was facing difficulties with the IDE and Browser in order to provide my website a responsive view. I also came to know about Bootstrap which speeded up things real fast for me and I shifted from Dreamweaver to Open source Software i.e. Aptana Studio 3. I also got acquainted with Pixlr to provide my website a good design where video tutorials from youtube played a major part.
